At ThermoReLeaf we address the problem of crop yield losses due to heat exposure, a global threat to food security that has worsened in recent years due to climate change.
Problem

Heat waves are posing significant challenges for agriculture producers, affecting both their short-term profitability and long-term sustainability.

The agriculture market size is expected to grow from $13.27T in 2023 to $14.35T in 2024 at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.2%.
