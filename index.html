Heat Resistant Crops
Problem Solution Products Advantages Impact Team
At ThermoReLeaf we address the problem of crop yield losses due to heat exposure, a global threat to food security that has worsened in recent years due to climate change.
Problem

Heat waves are posing significant challenges for agriculture producers, affecting both their short-term profitability and long-term sustainability.

The agriculture market size is expected to grow from $13.27T in 2023 to $14.35T in 2024 at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.2%.

Our Solution

We make plants resistant to heat.

We offer a platform that combines small molecule discovery and gene editing to protect crops from heat stress.

These technologies are specifically designed to prevent the cell death events that take place in response to heat, avoiding the damage that occurs in plants exposed to high temperatures.

Products
Seeds of edited plants
Heat resilient plants obtained through DNA edition can cope with heat stress all along their life cycle.
AgBiological formulation
As editing is still not a viable solution for many relevant species in the global market, we protect them by supplying a specific formulation that allows plants to cope with extreme temperatures either by seed application or by foliar spray. This development allows a great adaptability to usual agricultural practices.
